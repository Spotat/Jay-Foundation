About us

Dedicated to everyone-Compassionate services for all people everywhere.

At The Jay Foundation, we are a non-profit organization born from a simple belief: every family deserves a safe place to call home and the resources to thrive. We focus specifically on supporting families living in slums and on the streets, who often face the harshest realities of poverty. Our team works tirelessly on the ground to understand the unique challenges these communities face, delivering direct aid and long-term solutions. We are more than just a charity; we are a community of compassionate individuals dedicated to transforming lives one family at a time.

Our Cause

Immediate Life-Saving Aid for Families in Crisis

We understand that hunger and exposure are immediate threats to the survival of street and slum families, which is why our Emergency Relief program operates 24/7 to deliver hot, nutritious meals, clean drinking water, and warm clothing directly to the most vulnerable locations. Our rapid response teams work tirelessly to identify families facing acute distress, providing not just food but also temporary shelter and medical first aid to stabilize their health before connecting them with long-term support services. By ensuring that no child or adult goes without basic sustenance, we lay the critical foundation for them to eventually rebuild their lives with dignity and hope.

Our Cause

Empowering the Next Generation Through Learning and Trade

Breaking the cycle of poverty requires more than just food; it demands access to quality education and practical vocational training that can lead to sustainable employment. Our Education and Skill Development initiative offers free tutoring, literacy classes, and creative workshops for children living in slums, while simultaneously providing adults with hands-on training in trades such as tailoring, carpentry, and basic electronics. We believe that by equipping families with the knowledge and skills to earn a living, we are not just teaching them a job but giving them the tools to secure a future where they can support themselves and their loved ones independently.

Our Cause

Comprehensive Medical Care and Hygiene Support for All

Lack of access to healthcare is a major barrier for street families, often turning minor illnesses into life-threatening conditions, so our Community Wellness program brings essential medical services directly to the communities we serve. We organize regular health camps staffed by volunteer doctors and nurses who provide free check-ups, vaccinations, maternal care, and treatment for common diseases, while also distributing hygiene kits and conducting sanitation workshops to prevent the spread of infection. By prioritizing physical and mental well-being, we ensure that families are healthy enough to work, learn, and thrive in their new environments.
